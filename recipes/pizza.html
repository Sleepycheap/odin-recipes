The Perfect Pizza

Description

Cheesy, melty, and delicious, this pizza recipe is sure to always please

Ingredients

  • 00 Flour
  • Mozzeralla Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Water
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Measure the dough
  2. Mix dough and water until proper consistency
  3. Strech dough into circle
  4. Cover dough circle in sauce to where the crust should be
  5. Cover liberally with cheese
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes
  7. Cover with basil and serve
