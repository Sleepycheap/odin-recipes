Sinful Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolately, moist, and positvely amazing, this cake will beat all your other cakes

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate
  • Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Mix dry Ingredients
  2. Slowly add in wet Ingredients
  3. Pour into cake tin
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
