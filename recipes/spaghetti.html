Stunning Spaghetti

Description

This red sauce uses a special blend of herbs that are certain to make this a favorite

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spices
  • Meat
  • Crushed Tomatoes

steps

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Brown the meat
  3. Add crushed Tomatoes to meat
  4. Add spices to Tomatoes
  5. Let simmer until thick
